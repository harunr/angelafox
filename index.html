Therapy that gets to the heart of the matter

Often our problems are more deep-rooted than we realise and thus require tried and tested, evidence-based psychological therapies in order to recognise and resolve the issues in question. I use primarily Emotionally Focussed models of therapy as they are based on 30 years of research and are proven to be successful... Read More + Read less - Additionally, Attachment theory, which is based on 50 years of research underpins my work. Couples Therapy based on Emotionally Focussed Therapy has achieved a 75% success rate with couples. Fully 86% of couples report feeling happier in their relationships. And results are long-lasting. There are only a few professionals trained in Emotionally Focussed Therapy in South Africa.

My therapy is centred on working with the deeper emotions that underly your behaviour and experience, whether you are a couple, an individual, a child, a teen or a family. It connects with the heart of your experience and resolves the root of your difficulties so that you can move forward in your life with greater compassion and insight.
